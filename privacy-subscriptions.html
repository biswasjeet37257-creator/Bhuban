Ads on Bhuban

You may see ads on Bhuban based on general factors, like the topic of a video. The ads you see may also depend on your choices on this page and other sites. To learn more about how ads work for family audiences, visit the Google for Families Help Center.

Ad preferences are managed through your Google account settings. Bhuban uses contextual advertising to show relevant ads based on video content.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Bhuban, we collect information to provide you with the best video sharing experience.

Information You Provide

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Profile information (avatar, bio, channel description)
  • Videos you upload (content, titles, descriptions, thumbnails)
  • Comments, likes, and interactions
  • Messages and communications

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (browser, OS, device type)
  • Usage data (videos watched, watch time, interactions)
  • IP address and location data
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our video platform services
  • Personalize your content recommendations
  • Process video uploads and streaming
  • Enable social features (comments, subscriptions, likes)
  • Send notifications about your account and content
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Video Content and Analytics

When you upload or watch videos on Bhuban:

  • We track view counts, watch time, and engagement metrics
  • Video analytics help creators understand their audience
  • We may process videos for quality optimization (HD, 2K, 4K)
  • Thumbnails and metadata are stored for content discovery
  • AI features may analyze content for recommendations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Other Users: Your public profile, videos, and comments are visible to others
  • Service Providers: Cloud storage, analytics, and infrastructure partners
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login and preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content recommendations
  • Measure advertising effectiveness (if applicable)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
Note: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a readable format

8. Children's Privacy

Bhuban is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party services:

  • Google OAuth for authentication

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

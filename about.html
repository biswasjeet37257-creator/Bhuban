Our Mission

Bhuban was created to empower creators and delight viewers with a premium video-sharing experience. We believe in:

  • Quality First: Every video deserves to be seen in the best possible quality.
  • Creator Freedom: Your content, your rules. We support creators with tools and analytics.
  • Innovation: From AI assistants to shorts, we're always pushing boundaries.
  • Community: Building connections between creators and viewers worldwide.

Technology Stack

Bhuban is built with cutting-edge technology:

  • Frontend: Pure HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript with glassmorphism design
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB
  • AI: Google Generative AI for intelligent features
  • Authentication: JWT + Google OAuth 2.0
  • Streaming: Optimized video delivery with range requests

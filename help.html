Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a video?
Click the upload button in the header, select your video file, add a title and description, then click "Upload Video". Your video will be processed and published to your channel.
How do I change my profile picture?
Go to your profile settings by clicking your avatar, then select "Profile Settings". Click on your current profile picture to upload a new one.
How do I delete a video?
Go to Creator Studio, click on "Content", find the video you want to delete, click the three dots menu, and select "Delete". Confirm the deletion.
How do I subscribe to a channel?
Visit any channel page or watch a video, then click the "Subscribe" button below the video or on the channel page. You'll receive notifications about new uploads.
How do I enable dark mode?
Go to Settings, find the "Appearance" section, and select "Dark" from the theme dropdown. Your preference will be saved automatically.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Click the three dots menu on any video, select "Report", choose the reason for reporting, and submit. Our team will review the content.
